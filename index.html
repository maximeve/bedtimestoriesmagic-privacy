3. Information we collect

3.1 Information you provide

  • Account information used for sign-in (for example Apple or Google account details shared by your provider).

  • Messages you send when contacting support.

3.2 Information collected automatically

  • Anonymous technical identifiers used for guest free-tier limits and fraud prevention.
  • Usage data needed to provide subscription and credit entitlement status.

3.3 Purchases

Payments are processed by Apple App Store. We receive purchase state and transaction identifiers through RevenueCat and our backend so we can activate subscriptions and credit packs.

3.4 Generated content

Story prompts are processed by our AI providers to generate text, audio, and images. Generated stories are stored locally on your device unless otherwise stated in-app.

4. How we use information

  • Provide and improve the app features.
  • Authenticate users and manage free usage, credits, and subscriptions.
  • Process purchases and restore entitlements.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent abuse.

5. Children

Bedtime Stories Magic is intended to be used by parents or guardians for children. If you believe personal data has been submitted inappropriately, contact us and we will review and delete data where required by law.

6. Sharing with service providers
